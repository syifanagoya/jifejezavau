    Early life

    The legendary racing driver was born on 3rd January 1987, in Heppenheim, Germany to his parents Heike Vettel and Norbert Vettel. There is not much information about his family background.     Career

    Sebastian started racing when he was only three and a half years old. By the age of 10, he was already ahead of his peers. At 11, he made a place in Red Bull’s junior team. For the next six years, he remained with Red Bull and bagged 18 titles out of 20. This was a significant achievement for a teenager boy. At that time, he was only 18 years old but has impressed the world with his exceptional driving skills. In 2006, he turned a professional formula one driver and came together with BMW Sauber. However, his contract with BMW Sauber lasted only for one year.

    After a few years, he again signed the contract with Red Bull in 2009 and remained with it till 2015. During these years, he won 4 world championships consecutively in 2010, 2011, 2012, and 2013. Following that, he garnered massive publicity and was regarded as the greatest formula one driver in the world. In 2015, he broke the contract with Red Bull and came together with Ferrari. His contract with Ferrari is until 2020.

    Awards & achievements

    Throughout his career, Sebastian has won several championships and titles other than winning four world championships. Talking about honours, he has been awarded ‘Silver Laurel Leaf Award’, one of the most prestigious awards of Germany. He has also won DHL Fastest Lap Award three times.
