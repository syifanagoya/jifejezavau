    Meet Hack My Home star Jessica Banks

    Jessica Banks (age: 51, born in 1972) is an American inventor, artist, and businesswoman based in Brooklyn, New York. As the founder of RockPaperRobot, a company specializing in kinetic furniture and lighting, she has revolutionized the concept of traditional decor by incorporating principles of physics and engineering.

    Recently Banks joined the entertainment industry with a Netflix show – “Hack My Home,” which landed on the OTT platform on July 7, 2023. The all-new reality show “Hack My Home” features four real estate experts who try to help people reorganize their homes without needing renovation.

    The four visionaries we are talking about include Ati Williams, who looks after the construction part; Jessica takes care of any technological modifications; Mikel Welch, who looks after the interior decoration and Brooks Atwood, who provides out-of-the-box ideas. Overall the show is a must-watch for home lovers.
