    FAQs about Rudolf Jansen

    What was Jansen known for?

    He was renowned for his accompaniment in Lied recitals and chamber music, and was a prominent figure in classical music as a teacher and performer.

    With which famous singers did Jansen collaborate

    He collaborated with notable singers like Elly Ameling, Barbara Bonney, Dietrich Fischer-Dieskau, and many others.

    Where did Jansen study and teach?

    He studied and taught at the Conservatorium van Amsterdam and also taught at the Musikhochschule Nürnberg.

    Did Jansen receive any awards during his career?

    Yes, he received several awards including the Edison Award, Toonkunst Jubileumprijs, and Zilveren Vriendenkrans.

    What recordings did Jansen make?

    Jansen recorded over 120 CDs, focusing primarily on Lied recitals, including complete songs by composers like Alphons Diepenbrock and Edvard Grieg.

    When did Jansen pass away?

    He passed away on February 12, 2024, at the age of 84.

    What was the last concert of Jansen?

    Did Jansen contribute to music education?
